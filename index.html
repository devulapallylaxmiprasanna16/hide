About HIDE

HIDE (Hands-on Innovation, Design & Engineering) is an initiative under S.K Technologies based in Hanumakonda, focused on delivering practical, hands-on training, internships, and innovation support for engineering and diploma students.

Unlike theory-heavy programs, we offer real product building and student mentorship through internships, workshops, and live projects.

We guide students from idea to final product with expert mentorship, project support, and industry-relevant tools.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between academic learning and practical industry skills.
